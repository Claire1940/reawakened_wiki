## Core Progression and Stat Mechanics

Progression in Reawakened is multi-layered. Unlike standard RPGs where you simply watch a level number increase, here you must balance your base level, your hunter rank, and your specific class skills. Every time you level up, you earn stat points that are vital for shaping your combat effectiveness.

The stat system is designed to reward specialization. Once you reach 100 points in a specific attribute, you unlock unique perks that fundamentally change how your character interacts with the world.

| Stat | Primary Benefit | 100-Point Perk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Agility** | Increases movement speed and dodge window | **Teleportation** (Instant repositioning) |
| **Strength** | Increases raw physical damage | **Posture Damage** (Easier to guard-break) |
| **Vitality** | Increases maximum HP and defense | **Damage Reduction** (Passive tanking) |
| **Intelligence** | Increases mana pool and skill damage | **Cooldown Reduction** (Faster skill cycling) |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not spread your points too thin early on. It is generally more effective to rush 100 points in either Agility or Strength to unlock your first major perk before diversifying.

## Choosing Your Path: PvE vs. PvP

One of the most innovative features in the game is the Path system. This choice, made early in your journey, determines what kind of content will be your primary source of income and experience. While a **reawakened script** can help automate the "grind" of these paths, choosing the one that fits your personal playstyle is crucial for long-term enjoyment.

1.  **Chairman Path:** This is the ultimate choice for PvE enthusiasts. It focuses on leading rescue missions into dangerous gates and managing mining operations to gather rare ores.
2.  **Assassin Path:** Specifically designed for PvP players. You can pick up player contracts from the quest board, allowing you to hunt down other players in your server for high-stakes rewards.
3.  **Hunter Path:** This path bridges the gap between normal leveling and endgame raiding. It focuses on subjugation missions against massive bosses rather than standard mobs.

## Gates, Dungeons, and Rare Traits

The heart of the Solo Leveling experience lies in the Gates. In Reawakened, there are eight different gate ranks. Clearing these requires you to defeat every mob within the instance before facing a final boss.

Higher-ranked gates, particularly **Red Gates** and **Secret Dungeons**, offer the best rewards but come with a significant jump in difficulty. This is where your "Trait" comes into play. Traits are secondary powers, such as the **Goliath Trait**, which provide massive passive buffs or unique active abilities. There are currently seven traits available, and rolling a high-tier trait is often the difference between a successful S-rank run and a failed mission.

## Gear and Equipment Optimization

Unlike many Roblox games where gear is just a cosmetic skin, in Reawakened, equipment is class-specific. An Assassin wearing Heavy Plate armor will lose out on vital Agility scaling, while a Mage needs robes that boost Mana regeneration.

To optimize your build:
1.  **Match Gear to Class:** Look for prefixes that align with your primary stats (e.g., "Swift" for Agility builds).
2.  **Focus on Posture:** In 2026, the meta revolves around posture damage. Ensure your weapon has a high posture-break stat to stun bosses.
3.  **Upgrade Consistently:** Use the resources gathered from the Chairman path to enhance your gear at the blacksmith.
