## Choosing the Best Class After Redeeming Codes

Once you have used your **reawakened codes** to secure Class Reserves, you might be tempted to switch your current build. Choosing the right class is vital for both PvE (Player vs. Environment) and PvP (Player vs. Player) success.

### Warrior
The Warrior is a balanced class with excellent skill sets. It is highly recommended for players who enjoy a mix of offensive pressure and survivability. It remains one of the most consistent choices for clearing mid-tier dungeons.

### Tank
Currently, the Tank class boasts some of the highest "M1" (basic attack) damage in the game. In many PvP scenarios, a well-geared Tank can defeat opponents in just a few hits. Their high health pool makes them indispensable for group dungeon raids.

### Assassin
The Assassin is specialized for PvP. With high mobility and burst damage, they can weave in and out of combat. However, be aware that in the current meta, some players utilize "infinite running" tactics, which can make the Assassin's job more difficult if you don't have high Agility.

### Mage and Healer
The Mage is often considered weak in the early game because their first few abilities lack significant impact. However, they scale incredibly well into the late game. The Healer is the ultimate support class; while difficult to play solo, they are the backbone of any successful high-rank party.

## Understanding Stats and Milestones

In Reawakened, stats do more than just increase your numbers. Reaching a milestone of **100 points** in any specific stat unlocks a unique passive or active ability that can fundamentally change how you play.

*   **Strength:** The primary damage source for *all* classes, including Mages. Reaching 100 Strength grants significantly increased posture damage, allowing you to break through enemy defenses faster.
*   **Agility:** Reduces skill cooldowns. At 100 Agility, players unlock the **Teleport** ability, which is essential for dodging boss mechanics and closing gaps in PvP.
*   **Intelligence:** Increases your mana pool. The 100-point milestone grants a passive that restores mana every time you land a successful attack.
*   **Vitality:** Boosts your total HP. At 100 Vitality, your attacks gain the ability to "block break" through an opponent's parries, making you an unstoppable force.

## Fast Leveling Strategies

After you've redeemed your **reawakened codes**, your next goal is to hit the level cap as quickly as possible. The most efficient way to do this is through the Daily Quest system and the Subjugation Quest Board.

### The Daily Quest Routine
Every day, you can perform a series of physical exercises by pressing the **T** key. These include:
*   **Push-ups:** Click the keys as they reach the circle.
*   **Setups:** Press 'A' when the line hits the green bar.
*   **Squats:** Press 'E' on the blue bar.
*   **Running:** Simply travel a certain distance on foot.

Upon completion, you are given a choice between +5 levels or a **Secret Key**. Expert players suggest always choosing the **Secret Key**. While levels are easy to farm through combat, Secret Keys are rare and required for late-game "Demon Dungeons" which offer the best loot in the game.

### Quest Board Prioritization
When visiting the HQ, you will find three main types of missions. Not all are created equal when it comes to time efficiency.

1.  **Subjugation (Best):** These missions involve killing specific mobs in the open world. Even at a low rank, D-rank subjugations offer massive XP for very little effort.
2.  **Gates (Good for Gear):** These are dungeon clears. While they take longer, they are the only source for blueprints used in crafting high-tier weapons and armor.
3.  **Extraction (Avoid):** These missions require you to teleport to a different server and rescue an NPC. They are generally considered too time-consuming for the rewards they provide.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Reawakened is a parry-based game. If your ping is high (above 200ms), you will struggle to parry effectively. In these cases, focus on high Vitality and Tank builds to soak up damage instead of relying on perfect timing.

## Crafting and Resource Gathering

To get the best gear, you must interact with the crafting NPC at the HQ. You cannot simply find "Legendary" swords on the ground; you must earn the recipe and gather the materials.

Recipes are obtained as rare drops from **Gate** missions. Once you have a recipe, you will need to mine crystals found inside dungeons. To do this, ensure you have a pickaxe equipped in your inventory. While it may be tempting to mine every crystal you see, it is often more efficient to clear the dungeon first and then go back for the resources to ensure you don't run out of time on the mission clock.
