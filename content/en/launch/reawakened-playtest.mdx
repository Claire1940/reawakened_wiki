## Gameplay Strategies for Playtesters

Participating in a **reawakened playtest** is about more than just playing; it is about stress-testing the systems. To provide the best feedback (and have the most fun), follow these strategic approaches.

### Tactical Deck-Building (DR2)
In *The Reawakening*, focus on "breaking" the balance. If you find a card combination that seems too powerful, document it. The developers specifically want to see where the "broken" interactions lie so they can tune them before the full 2026 release.

*   **Minion Stacking:** Try to summon the maximum number of minions allowed to test frame rate stability.
*   **Resource Hoarding:** See if you can bypass progression pacing by hoarding specific currency types early on.

### Survival Optimization (Roblox)
For the open-world version, focus on the environment and player-to-player interactions.

*   **Map Boundaries:** Explore the edges of the map to find "leaks" or untextured areas.
*   **Combat Latency:** Engage in PvP or PvE encounters during high-population hours to test how the server handles lag.

> **💡 Tip:** Always use the in-game feedback tools or "Bug Report" channels in Discord. Developers prioritize players who provide structured data over those who simply report "the game is hard."
