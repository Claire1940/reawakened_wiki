## Core Progression and Leveling Overhaul

The 2026 updates have completely reimagined how players interact with the world. No longer is leveling a matter of mindless grinding; instead, the **reawakened patch notes** introduce a variety of mission types that keep the gameplay loop fresh and rewarding. The quest board is now the central hub for all hunter activities, offering three distinct mission types that cater to different playstyles.

### New Quest Board Mission Types

| Mission Type | Objective | Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate** | Search the environment for clues and hidden items. | High EXP, Rare Materials |
| **Clear Gate** | Enter a dungeon, defeat mobs, and slay the boss. | Balanced EXP, Gear Drops |

## Stat System and Perk Unlocks

One of the most significant changes mentioned in the **reawakened patch notes** is the "Perk Milestone" system. Every time you level up, you receive one stat point to invest in Agility, Strength, Vitality, or Intelligence. However, the real power spike occurs when a stat reaches 100 points, unlocking a unique passive or active ability.

### Stat Perk Milestones

| Stat | Perk Unlock (at 100 Points) | Combat Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Agility** | **Teleport** | Instant short-range blink for evasion. |
| **Strength** | **Posture Breaker** | Significantly increases posture damage to bosses. |
| **Vitality** | **Endurance Soul** | Massive boost to health regeneration and damage reduction. |
| **Intelligence** | **Mana Flow** | Reduces cooldowns and increases skill damage. |

By specializing in specific stats, players can now create "builds" rather than all-rounder characters. A high-agility Assassin plays fundamentally differently than a high-vitality Tank, providing a level of depth rarely seen in Roblox RPGs.

## Choosing Your Path: PvE vs. PvP

The introduction of the "Path" system is perhaps the most discussed feature in the recent **reawakened patch notes**. Upon starting or reaching a specific milestone, players must choose one of three paths. This choice dictates the type of exclusive content and rewards you can access.

### Path Comparison Table

| Path | Primary Focus | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chairman** | PvE & Resource Management | Lead rescue missions and mining runs for rare ores. |
| **Assassin** | PvP & Bounty Hunting | Take player contracts to hunt others for rewards. |
| **Hunter** | Boss Subjugation | Exclusive access to high-tier boss battles and traits. |

Choosing the Chairman path is ideal for players who enjoy cooperative play and economic progression. The Assassin path, however, turns the game into a high-stakes survival experience where you can earn massive rewards by defeating other players. The Hunter path remains the "classic" Solo Leveling experience, focusing on scaling power to defeat the world's most dangerous monsters.

## The Six Combat Classes

While Paths define your "career," Classes define your combat style. There are currently six classes available in Reawakened, each featuring seven unique skills. This variety ensures that even within a specific Path, two players can have completely different combat rotations.

1.  **Warrior:** The quintessential melee DPS, focusing on heavy sword strikes and area-of-effect (AoE) damage.
2.  **Tank:** Built for the frontline, using shields and taunts to protect allies in high-rank gates.
3.  **Assassin:** High burst damage and mobility, perfect for the Assassin Path.
4.  **Mage:** Ranged elemental damage that can clear entire rooms of mobs instantly.
5.  **Healer:** Essential for S-rank gates, providing health and mana restoration to the party.
6.  **Striker:** A martial-arts focused class that relies on fast combos and stuns.

## Gate Ranks and Dungeons

The core of the game revolves around clearing Gates. According to the latest **reawakened patch notes**, there are now eight distinct gate ranks, ranging from E-rank (beginner) to the terrifying S+ rank. Each gate features a unique environment, enemy types, and a final boss that must be defeated to "close" the gate.

> **Warning:** Red Gates are unpredictable. They can appear randomly and often contain enemies far stronger than the initial gate rank suggests. Never enter a Red Gate without a full party and plenty of supplies.

### Gate Difficulty and Loot Scaling

| Gate Rank | Difficulty Level | Notable Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E to D Rank** | Easy | Basic gear, low-tier ores. |
| **C to B Rank** | Moderate | Class-specific weapons, mid-tier traits. |
| **A to S Rank** | Hard | Legendary armor, S-rank skill scrolls. |
| **Red Gates** | Extreme | Secret Dungeons, Goliath Trait, Ancient Ores. |

The environment within these gates is procedurally influenced, meaning no two runs feel exactly the same. The 2026 update has also improved the AI of gate bosses, making them use complex patterns that require players to utilize their Agility perks and class skills effectively.

## Gear, Traits, and Customization

Beyond stats and classes, your hunter's power is heavily influenced by their equipment and traits. Traits act as a secondary power system, providing passive buffs or active "ultimate" abilities. Gear is now class-locked, meaning you won't find yourself accidentally equipping a Healer staff on a Warrior. This change encourages players to trade with others or focus on specific mining runs to craft the perfect set for their build.
