## S-Tier: The Meta Kings

### Assassin
The Assassin currently reigns supreme at the top of the **reawakened tier list**. This class is defined by its ability to melt boss health bars in seconds using high-impact burst combos. In the 2026 meta, mobility is the best form of defense, and the Assassin’s kit allows players to weave in and out of combat effortlessly. While it requires precision to master the "Follow-up" attacks (activated by pressing Control), the payoff is a character that can solo D-rank and C-rank dungeons with ease.

## A-Tier: Powerhouse Contenders

### Mage
If your goal is efficient grinding and raid dominance, the Mage is your best bet. Unlike other RPGs where Mages are fragile, the Reawakened Mage utilizes powerful Area of Effect (AoE) spells that make clearing "Subjugation" missions a breeze. 

One unique aspect of the Mage in this game is that its damage scales with Strength, similar to melee classes. While Intelligence is still required for Mana pool depth, the raw power comes from physical training. The only drawback is a slight disadvantage in close-quarters PvP, where faster classes can interrupt long casting animations.

## B-Tier: The Reliable All-Rounders

### Warrior
The Warrior is the gold standard for beginners. It offers a forgiving learning curve with simple, reliable moves that don't require pixel-perfect timing. With solid defense and respectable damage, it is the safest choice for players who want to experience all content without feeling underpowered.

### Tank
Surprisingly, the Tank has emerged as a hidden gem in 2026. Recent player testing has shown that the Tank possesses the highest M1 (basic attack) damage in the game. In a direct trade of blows, a Tank can often "four-tap" lighter classes like the Striker. However, their slower movement speed makes them reliant on teammates or perfect parries to close the gap against ranged opponents.

## Understanding Stats and Breakpoints

In Reawakened, your stats do more than just increase numbers; they unlock passive abilities once you hit specific thresholds. Reaching 100 points in any stat provides a "Special Effect" that can fundamentally change your playstyle.

| Stat | Effect at 100 Points | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | **Increased Posture Damage** | All Classes (Primary Damage Source) |
| **Agility** | **Unlock Teleport / Lower Cooldowns** | Assassins & Strikers |
| **Intelligence** | **Mana Regen on Attack** | Mages & Skill-Spammers |
| **Vitality** | **Block Break through Parries** | Warriors & Tanks |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore Strength even if you are playing a Mage. Strength is the universal damage source for all classes in the current version of the game.

## Career Paths: Hunter vs. Assassin vs. Chairman

After completing your initial quests and reaching the HQ, you will be asked to choose a career path. This choice dictates how you will earn experience and interact with the world.

1.  **Hunter (Recommended):** This is the premier PvE path. It focuses on killing monsters and clearing gates. It is widely considered the fastest way to level up because you stay in the same server and focus on combat efficiency.
2.  **Assassin:** Focused on PvP. While lucrative for skilled players, the 2026 PvP meta can be frustrating due to "infinite running" tactics where opponents avoid combat entirely.
3.  **Chairman:** Involves rescuing NPCs. This path is generally avoided by the community as it requires frequent server hopping and offers lower XP rewards compared to the Hunter path.

## Leveling Strategy and Mission Types

To climb the ranks and improve your standing on the **reawakened tier list**, you must engage with the Quest Board. Not all missions are created equal, and focusing on the wrong ones can stall your progress.

*   **Subjugation (Best):** These missions require you to hunt specific mobs. They offer the best XP-to-time ratio. Even at low levels, D-rank Subjugations are soloable if you master the parry mechanic.
*   **Gate Clearing:** Essential for gear progression. Gates are the only source for blueprints, which are required to craft high-tier weapons and armor. 
*   **Extraction:** Generally ignored. These missions take too long and involve teleporting to separate dungeon instances for mediocre rewards.

## Crafting and Gear Progression

You cannot simply buy the best gear in Reawakened; you must earn it. The crafting system requires you to find recipes (blueprints) within dungeons and then gather materials like crystals and monster drops.

1.  **Acquire Recipe:** Run Gates until a blueprint drops for your specific class.
2.  **Gather Materials:** Use a pickaxe to mine highlighted crystals inside dungeons.
3.  **Visit the HQ Smith:** Talk to the crafting NPC to assemble your gear. Note that higher rarity weapons have significantly lower success rates or much harder material requirements.

## Combat Mechanics: The Art of the Parry

Regardless of where your class sits on the **reawakened tier list**, your survival depends on mastering the combat system. 

*   **Blocking (F):** Holding F reduces incoming damage but drains your posture.
*   **Parrying (Tap F):** Tapping F right as an attack lands negates damage and opens the enemy up for a counter.
*   **Strong Attack (R):** Useful for breaking an enemy's guard.
*   **Slam Attack (Space + R):** A great way to start an engagement or deal AoE damage to grouped mobs.

> **💡 Tip:** If you have high ping (200ms+), parrying becomes extremely difficult. In these cases, focus on a high-Vitality Tank build that can absorb hits rather than relying on perfect timing.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best class for a solo player in Reawakened?**

A: The Assassin is currently the best for solo players due to its high burst damage and mobility, allowing you to take down bosses without needing a dedicated healer or tank.

**Q: How do I get more Skill Points?**

A: Skill points are earned by leveling up. The fastest way to level is by completing "Subjugation" missions and finishing your Daily Quests (Push-ups, Setups, Squats, and Running).

**Q: Why should I choose the Secret Key over +5 Levels?**

A: While levels are easy to farm through combat, Secret Keys are rare and required for late-game "Demon Dungeons." Always prioritize the Secret Key when given the choice during Daily Quest rewards.

**Q: Is the reawakened tier list likely to change soon?**

A: The developers are active in balancing the game. While Assassin and Mage are top-tier now, upcoming patches are expected to buff the Striker and Healer classes to make them more viable for solo play.
