## Choosing Your Difficulty and Character

Before you even set foot in the Town of Grove, you must decide the level of challenge you want to face. Unlike many modern RPGs, your choice here is permanent for that character. Higher difficulties don't just make enemies tougher; they also scale the quality of the items you can find, rewarding the brave with superior gear.

| Difficulty | Description | Item Quality Potential |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Squire** | The easiest mode, perfect for a relaxed experience or RPG newcomers. | Standard |
| **Adventurer** | The "Normal" experience. Balanced for those familiar with the genre. | Superior |
| **Hero** | Enemies are significantly tougher. Requires knowledge of FATE mechanics. | Exceptional |
| **Legend** | A brutal challenge where every encounter can be lethal. | Flawless |
| **Hardcore** | Legendary difficulty with permadeath enabled. One mistake and it's over. | Flawless |

Once you've selected your difficulty, you must choose a race. While FATE allows for flexible builds, your starting race provides a slight nudge toward specific playstyles.

| Race | Primary Bonus | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Human** | Balanced Stats | Any build / Indecisive players |
| **Shadow Elf** | Dexterity | Archers and Rogues |
| **Half-Orc** | Strength | Melee Warriors |
| **Cogger** | Critical Chance / Dodge | Specialized DPS builds |
| **Imp** | Magic / Mana | Mages and Spellcasters |

> **💡 Tip:** Don't stress too much about your race choice. In FATE, your gear and stat point allocation during level-ups will define your power more than your starting bonuses.

These books are superior to individual scrolls because they can hold up to 20 charges, saving precious inventory space.

### Stat Point Allocation
Every time you level up, you receive points to distribute across four main attributes. Balancing these is the secret to a successful **reawakened beginner guide** strategy:

*   **Strength:** Increases physical damage and is required for heavy armor and melee weapons.
*   **Dexterity:** Improves your hit chance and dodge rate. Essential for bows and daggers.
*   **Vitality:** Boosts your total Health and Stamina. Everyone needs some Vitality to survive boss bursts.
*   **Magic:** Increases Mana and spell damage. Required to learn powerful high-level spells.

## The Art of Fishing and Gold Farming

Fishing is not just a minigame in FATE: Reawakened; it is a primary economic engine. You can fish in the town's pond or at sparkling spots found throughout the dungeon. Items caught while fishing often have higher sell values than standard dungeon loot, and you can even catch rare fish that transform your pet into powerful creatures like dragons or spiders.

To maximize your efficiency, follow these steps:
1.  **Clear a Floor:** Explore the dungeon floor and clear out enemies.
2.  **Locate Fishing Spots:** Look for the ripples in the water.
3.  **Sell the Loot:** Use your pet to run back to town to sell items while you keep fishing.

> **⚠️ Warning:** In the 2026 remaster, fishing time between bites can be slightly longer than in the original game. Patience is required, but the rewards are worth the wait.

## Questing and Fame Management

Quests are provided by NPCs with exclamation marks over their heads. These missions usually involve fetching an item, killing a specific boss, or delivering a package. Completing quests grants Gold, Experience, and **Fame**.

Fame is just as important as Experience. As your Fame grows, you earn "Renown" levels, which grant extra skill points and allow you to equip **Elite** or **Legendary** gear. These items are marked with a small medallion icon and offer significantly higher stats than their common counterparts.

### The "Cancel Quest" Strategy
A pro tip for any **reawakened beginner guide** is the quest-cancellation trick. If you accept a "Fetch Item" quest, the item will spawn on a specific dungeon floor. Once you find the item, you can choose to keep it or sell it for a high price instead of turning it in. Simply open your Journal (J) and cancel the quest. The item remains in your inventory, allowing you to use it for yourself or profit from its high resale value.

## The Enchantment System: Breaking the Game

Enchanting is where you turn a good character into a god-like force. NPCs like Riko in Grove offer enchantment services for a fee. Unlike other RPGs where items have strict limits, FATE allows you to stack dozens of enchantments on a single piece of gear.

| Enchantment Outcome | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Positive Buffs** | Adds stats like +10 Strength or 5% Life Steal. |
| **Sockets** | Adds a slot for a Gem, allowing for further customization. |
| **Unique (Blue)** | Rare, high-powered modifiers that are extremely valuable. |
| **Wipe** | Removes all previous enchantments (use with caution!). |
| **Negative Debuffs** | Occasionally adds a penalty (e.g., -5 Vitality). |

To succeed with enchanting in 2026, wait until you have a solid gold reserve. The cost of enchanting increases with the item's value and the number of existing enchantments. Start with a high-base-value item (10,000+ gold) to ensure that even a mediocre enchantment roll increases the item's worth.

## Pet Management and Combat

Your pet is your most loyal companion, acting as both a tank and a mobile storage unit. In the **reawakened beginner guide** context, remember that your pet can equip two rings, one necklace, and an earring. 

> **💡 Tip:** To heal your pet in the remaster, you must click the pet's portrait in the top-left corner. This will automatically use a potion from your inventory.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my character's appearance later in the game?**

A: No. Character appearance and race are locked once created. However, the armor you equip will change your visual look as you progress through the dungeon.

**Q: What happens if I run out of Town Portal scrolls?**

A: If you run out of scrolls and don't have the Town Portal spell, you will have to walk back up through the stairs of every floor you cleared. Always keep your Town Portal Book filled to avoid this tedious trek.

**Q: Is there a maximum floor limit in FATE: Reawakened?**

A: Technically, no. The dungeons in this **reawakened beginner guide** are procedurally generated and can go on for thousands of levels. Most players consider the game "beaten" after defeating the main quest boss, but the infinite dungeon allows for endless play.

**Q: How do I identify magical items without spending gold?**

A: While scrolls and books cost gold, you can eventually find or buy the "Identify" spell. Investing points into Charm Magic will make this spell more reliable, allowing you to identify all your loot for free using only Mana.
