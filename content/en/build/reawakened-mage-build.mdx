## Mage Class Overview: Power and Fragility

The Mage is one of the six core classes available in Reawakened. Unlike the Tank or the Warrior, the Mage does not rely on physical bulk or balanced stats. Instead, it utilizes a vast mana pool to unleash seven unique skills that can clear entire rooms of enemies in seconds. However, this power comes at a cost: Mages are notoriously fragile.

If you are following a **reawakened mage build**, your primary goal is to maintain distance. You have the highest raw damage potential in the game, but even a few hits from a high-rank boss can end your run. Mastering the rhythm of spellcasting and mana management is vital for survival in high-level gates.

## Optimal Stat Allocation for Mages

In Reawakened, every level grants you a stat point that can be invested into Agility, Strength, Vitality, or Intelligence. For a **reawakened mage build**, Intelligence is your most important stat, as it directly scales your spell damage and mana capacity. However, blindly dumping every point into Intelligence can leave you vulnerable.

The game features a perk system where reaching 100 points in a specific stat unlocks a game-changing ability. For Mages, two stats are particularly noteworthy:

1.  **Intelligence:** Increases total mana and spell damage. Reaching 100 points unlocks advanced mana regeneration and potency perks.
2.  **Agility:** Increases movement speed and dodge capability. Reaching **100 Agility unlocks Teleport**, which is arguably the most important survival tool for a Mage.

> **💡 Tip:** Prioritize reaching 100 Intelligence first to maximize your damage, then focus on Agility until you unlock Teleport to solve your survivability issues.

| Stat | Priority | Perk at 100 Points |
|---|---|---|
| **Intelligence** | **Primary** | Enhanced Spell Potency / Mana Regen |
| **Agility** | **Secondary** | **Teleport** (Crucial for kiting) |
| **Vitality** | **Tertiary** | Increased Health Pool |
| **Strength** | **Low** | Posture Damage (Mostly for melee) |

## Skills and Combat Strategy

Every class in Reawakened comes equipped with seven distinct skills. As a Mage, these skills range from long-range projectiles to massive Area of Effect (AoE) explosions. Your combat loop should revolve around "kiting"—the act of attacking while moving backward to keep enemies out of melee range.

Because your **reawakened mage build** relies on mana, you must monitor your blue bar constantly. If you run out of mana in the middle of a Red Gate, you lose your ability to defend yourself. Use your lower-cost "poke" spells for standard enemies and save your high-cost "ultimates" for bosses and large waves.

### The Importance of Teleport
Once you reach 100 Agility, your playstyle changes completely. Teleport allows you to instantly reposition when an enemy closes the gap. In PvP or high-rank boss fights, this is the difference between a successful clear and a quick death. Always keep enough mana in reserve for at least two consecutive Teleports.

## Choosing Your Path: Hunter vs. Chairman

At the start of your journey, you must choose a Path. This system is separate from your class and determines the type of content you will focus on. For a **reawakened mage build**, the choice usually comes down to your interest in PvE versus Boss Hunting.

*   **Chairman (PvE/Economy):** This path focuses on leading rescue missions and mining rare ores. It is excellent for Mages who want to farm gear and resources safely.
*   **Hunter (Boss Specialist):** This path focuses on subjugation missions against powerful bosses. Since Mages have high single-target burst damage, the Hunter path allows you to shine in high-stakes battles.
*   **Assassin (PvP):** Focused on player contracts. While Mages can be strong in PvP, the fragile nature of the build makes this a high-risk choice for experienced players only.

| Path | Focus | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Chairman** | Mining & Rescue | Resource farming and safe leveling. |
| **Hunter** | Boss Subjugation | High-skill players wanting the best loot. |
| **Assassin** | Player Contracts | Competitive PvP enthusiasts. |

## Gear and Traits for the Ultimate Mage

Gear in Reawakened is class-specific, meaning you should always look for armor and weapons that boost Intelligence and Mana. Do not just equip the item with the highest defense; look for "Mage" tags that provide bonuses to spell casting speed or elemental damage.

In addition to gear, you can acquire **Traits**. There are seven total traits in the game, such as the "Goliath" trait. For a Mage, traits that provide mana cost reduction or increased mobility are ideal. If you happen to roll a defensive trait like Goliath, it can help mitigate your low natural health, making your **reawakened mage build** much more forgiving in difficult dungeons.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid gear that heavily penalizes Agility. Even with high Intelligence, a Mage that cannot move is a dead Mage.

## Leveling and Gate Progression

To reach the endgame, you must master the Gate system. Gates range from E rank to S+ and include special Red Gates and Secret Dungeons. As a Mage, your AoE spells make you one of the fastest levelers in the game, as you can clear "Clear Gate" missions more efficiently than single-target classes like the Assassin.

1.  **Accept Quests:** Use the Quest Board for Investigate, Clear Gate, and Subjugation missions.
2.  **Daily Quests:** Complete the interactive mini-games (push-ups, squats) to earn extra rewards.
3.  **Rank Up:** Focus on reaching the next rank milestone to unlock harder gates with better gear drops.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best stat distribution for a reawakened mage build?**

A: The most effective distribution is a 2:1 ratio of Intelligence to Agility. Once you hit 100 Intelligence, put points into Agility until you hit 100 for the Teleport perk, then put the remaining points into Vitality for health.

**Q: Can Mages play solo in high-rank gates?**

A: Yes, Mages are excellent for solo play because their high damage allows them to kill enemies before they can get close. However, you must be very careful with your positioning, as you lack the health to survive multiple mistakes.

**Q: Which trait is best for a Mage?**

A: While traits are often random, any trait that increases mana regeneration or movement speed is top-tier. If you want more survivability, the Goliath trait is a strong secondary choice to help you survive boss hits.

**Q: How do I unlock more skills for my Mage?**

A: Skills are unlocked as you level up and rank up your character. By the time you reach the higher ranks (A or S), you will have access to all seven class skills, which you can hotkey for various combat situations.
