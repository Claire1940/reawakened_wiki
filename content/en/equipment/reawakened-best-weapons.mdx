## Class Synergies and Weapon Choices

In Reawakened, your weapon is often an extension of your class. The game features six distinct classes, each with a specific "feel" and mechanical requirement. To find the **reawakened best weapons** for your specific build, you must align your gear with your role.

### The Assassin's Edge: Daggers
The Assassin is currently the most popular class due to its high skill ceiling and resemblance to the series' protagonist. Daggers prioritize speed over health, allowing you to strike multiple times before an enemy can even trigger a parry animation. 

*   **Pros:** Fastest M1 (normal attack) speed, excellent for "chase down" scenarios in PvP.
*   **Cons:** Extremely fragile; a few missed parries can lead to an instant defeat.
*   **Best Stat:** Agility.

### The Frontline: Warrior and Tank
Warriors use Longswords to maintain a balance between offense and defense. They are versatile and can adapt to almost any dungeon scenario. Tanks, on the other hand, sacrifice all speed for pure power and durability. If you prefer soaking up damage while your teammates deal the killing blows, the Sword and Shield combo is your **reawakened best weapons** choice.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Playing as a Tank or Warrior requires precise timing on the "F" key for parrying. Because these classes move slower, you cannot simply dash away from every attack.

## Combat Mechanics: Mastering the Parry

Even the **reawakened best weapons** will fail you if you do not master the combat system. The game utilizes a posture bar and a parry window that many players find challenging due to slight input delays.

1.  **The Parry (F Key):** You must press the parry key roughly 0.5 to 1 second before the impact, depending on your server ping. Successful parries deplete the enemy's posture.
2.  **Posture Break:** Once an enemy's posture bar is empty, they are stunned, allowing you to unleash high-damage combos.
3.  **Dash and Mobility:** Use the dash to avoid unblockable "Red" attacks. For Assassins, dashing behind an enemy is the most effective way to start a dagger combo.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find the hitboxes inconsistent, try to focus on "M1 spam" only after a successful parry. Blindly swinging often leaves you open to a counter-attack that can drain 50% of your health.

## Daily Training: The Key to Weapon Scaling

In a direct nod to the source material, Reawakened features a daily quest system that is mandatory for long-term power. If you want your **reawakened best weapons** to hit their maximum damage potential, you must complete your daily physical regimen.

*   **25 Push-ups**
*   **25 Sit-ups**
*   **25 Squats**
*   **10km Run (implied through movement)**

Failure to complete these tasks can result in being sent to the "Punishment Zone," a dangerous area where you must survive against high-level mobs for a set duration. Consistently completing these dailies provides stat points that you can dump into Agility, Strength, or Intelligence to boost your weapon's effectiveness.

## Dungeon Strategies for 2026

Dungeons (or Gates) are the primary source of high-tier loot. However, soloing these gates is notoriously difficult. The ants and other monsters within have massive health pools that can overwhelm even a skilled Assassin.

*   **Party Up:** It is highly recommended to enter dungeons with at least one Healer or Tank.
*   **Mana Crystals:** Use your pickaxe to mine crystals within the dungeon. These are essential for upgrading your gear later in the game.
*   **Health Packs:** Keep an eye out for glowing green orbs dropped by enemies. These are your only source of healing mid-combat.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the reawakened best weapons for solo players?**

A: For solo players, the Longsword (Warrior) is generally considered the best due to its balanced stats. While Daggers have higher DPS, the Warrior's ability to survive mistakes makes it more forgiving during solo dungeon runs.

**Q: How do I change my weapon class?**

A: You can change your pathway by speaking to the different trainers in the main city, though this may reset certain progression milestones. Choose carefully before investing all your stat points into a specific build.

**Q: Are there legendary weapons in the current playtest?**

A: Yes, legendary weapons have a low drop rate (approximately 2%) from high-rank dungeon bosses. These weapons often feature unique active skills and superior stat scaling compared to common variants.

**Q: Why does the combat feel delayed?**

A: Reawakened is currently in a playtest phase. The developers are actively working on optimizing the parry windows and hitboxes. High ping (above 100ms) can significantly impact your ability to time blocks correctly.
