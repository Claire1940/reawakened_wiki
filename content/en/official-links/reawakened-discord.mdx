## Choosing Your Progression Path

Upon entering the world, you are faced with a choice that defines your gameplay loop. While most players gravitate toward the combat-heavy roles, each path offers a unique way to interact with the world.

| Path | Primary Focus | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Hunter** | Balanced combat and questing. | All-around players and beginners. |
| **Assassin** | High speed, critical damage, and stealth. | Solo players and PvP enthusiasts. |
| **Chairman (Miner)** | Resource gathering and economy. | Players who prefer crafting and trading. |

To unlock the Assassin path, you must speak with the Assassin Chairman. However, the game requires you to prove your worth as a basic Hunter first by completing initial subjugation quests. This involves hunting insectoids and proving your resolve to "The System."

## Mastering the Assassin Skill Tree

The Assassin build is currently the most popular meta choice due to its high burst damage. To become a true "Sung Jin Woo" clone, you must invest heavily in the Agility and Strength stats. Below are the core skills you will unlock as you progress through the Assassin class.

### Core Assassin Skills
1.  **Impale:** A foundational skill that delivers a high-damage stab. While simple, it is effective for interrupting NPC attack animations.
2.  **Fourfold Puncture:** A flurry of four rapid slashes. This is your primary DPS tool during dungeon boss fights.
3.  **Umbral Flash:** A mobility-based strike that allows you to move through enemies while dealing damage.
4.  **Mutilation:** A high-tier skill that deals massive AOE damage. Note that NPCs with high observation stats can parry this if timed poorly.
5.  **Phantom Blitz:** The ultimate gap closer. You teleport directly to your target for a devastating surprise attack.

## Reaching Level 100: Traits and Red Gates

The true endgame begins at level 100. This is where you can unlock your Trait, a passive ability that defines your ultimate power. Information on the exact requirements for these traits is often shared among veteran players on the **reawakened discord**.

### The Silencer Trait (Shadow Monarch)
To obtain the Silencer trait—the signature ability of the Shadow Monarch—you must complete a series of grueling challenges:
*   Solo an S-Rank Dungeon.
*   Solo a B-Rank Dungeon.
*   Complete an A-Rank Dungeon with a party of no more than two members.
*   Reach Wave 6 in the Secret Dungeon.

Once these are completed, you can roll for the Silencer trait. This trait provides the **Evade** passive, which removes aggro from all surrounding NPCs, and the **Assault** skill, which is an enhanced version of Mutilation.

### Crafting the Legendary Dual Daggers
The signature weapon of the endgame Assassin is the Dual Daggers. These are not dropped; they must be crafted using a blueprint found with a 3% chance in Red Gates.

| Material | Amount Required | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Eyes of Augre** | 15 | S-Rank Bosses |
| **Sword Essence** | 30 | Elite Humanoid Enemies |
| **Rune Fragments** | 50 | Extraction Missions |
| **Ice Shards** | 75 | Frost Gates |
| **Mana Crystals** | 140 | Mining & Dungeon Clearing |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The crafting grind for Dual Daggers is extremely time-consuming. It is highly recommended to use 2x Luck boosts obtained via codes from the **reawakened discord** when farming Red Gates for the blueprint.

## Dungeon Strategies for 2026

Dungeons are the primary source of XP and loot. In the current 2026 version of the game, NPC AI has been improved to parry and dodge more effectively.

*   **Goblins & Orcs:** These enemies tend to "sky" (jump around) frequently. Use skills like **Phantom Blitz** to lock them down.
*   **Humanoids:** These are the hardest to parry. Focus on using **Evade** or mobility skills to get behind them rather than trading hits head-on.
*   **Bosses:** Bosses like the Goblin King have massive HP bars. Vitality is a mandatory stat for these encounters; without at least 100 points in Vitality, most S-Rank bosses will one-shot you.

For more technical data on boss patterns, you can visit the [Official Roblox Developer Hub](https://www.roblox.com/groups/1) to see community-made combat logs and frame-data analysis.
