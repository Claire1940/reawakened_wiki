However, be aware that early-game Mage play can be frustrating until you unlock your third and fourth abilities, as the starter spells have long cooldowns and lower impact.

## Career Paths: Hunter vs. Assassin vs. Chairman

Once you complete the initial tutorial quests, you will be prompted to choose a Career Path. This is a separate system from your Class and determines what kind of missions you will primarily focus on.

*   **Hunter (Recommended):** This is the PvE-focused path. It is widely considered the best for leveling because it keeps you in the same server, hunting monsters and clearing dungeons. It provides the most consistent XP and gold.
*   **Assassin (PvP):** This path focuses on player-versus-player combat. While exciting, it is currently difficult to recommend for leveling because players can often run away indefinitely, making it an inefficient way to gain resources.
*   **Chairman:** This path involves rescuing NPCs across different servers. It is the least efficient path due to the constant server hopping and low XP rewards relative to the time invested.

> **💡 Tip:** Always choose the **Hunter** career path for your first character. It allows you to farm Subjugation missions and Gates much faster than the other options.

## Stat Milestones and Attribute Guide

In Reawakened, stats don't just increase your numbers—they unlock powerful passive abilities once they reach a threshold of 100 points. Interestingly, **Strength** is the primary damage stat for *all* classes, including Mages.

| Stat | 100 Point Milestone Bonus | Best For... |
|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | Increases Posture Damage | Everyone. This is your main source of damage. |
| **Agility** | Unlocks Teleport / Lower Cooldowns | Assassins and Strikers who need mobility. |
| **Intelligence** | Mana Regen on Basic Attacks | Mages and Healers to sustain spell casting. |
| **Vitality** | Block Break through Parries | Warriors and Tanks for frontline dominance. |

### How to Allocate Points
For most builds, you should focus on reaching 100 Strength as quickly as possible. This ensures your basic attacks (M1s) remain relevant while you wait for skill cooldowns. After hitting the Strength milestone, pivot to the stat that best supports your class (e.g., Intelligence for Mages or Agility for Assassins).

## Leveling Strategy: Subjugation and Gates

To reach the level required to unlock the **reawakened best class** skills, you must follow a specific questing loop.

1.  **Daily Quests:** Press **T** to access your training menu. Complete your push-ups, sit-ups, squats, and running. These provide massive XP boosts and are the easiest way to level up early.
2.  **Subjugation Missions:** Visit the Quest Board in the HQ. Subjugation missions (starting at D-Rank) are the most efficient way to farm XP. They require you to kill a set number of mobs in the open world.
3.  **Gate Clearing:** Gates are the game's dungeons. While they provide good XP, their primary purpose is dropping **Blueprints**. You cannot find gear directly; you must find the recipe and craft it at the HQ.
4.  **Avoid Extraction:** Extraction missions often require teleporting to different servers to rescue targets. They are time-consuming and offer lower rewards than simply farming Subjugation missions.

## Combat Mastery and Parrying

Reawakened uses a skill-based combat system that rewards timing over button-mashing. Even the **reawakened best class** will fail if the player doesn't master the parry mechanic.

*   **Blocking vs. Parrying:** Holding **F** blocks incoming damage but drains your posture. Tapping **F** right before an attack hits will perform a **Parry**, which stuns the enemy and leaves them open for a counter-attack.
*   **Shift Lock:** Press **Alt** to toggle shift lock. This is mandatory for landing skills and maintaining proper positioning during boss fights.
*   **Follow-up Attacks:** After launching an enemy with a heavy attack (**R**), you can press **Control** to perform a follow-up strike. Note: This mechanic can be buggy on high-ping servers, so use it with caution.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Reawakened is highly ping-sensitive. If your ping is above 200ms, parrying becomes nearly impossible. If you are lagging, focus on a **Tank** or **Warrior** build with high Vitality to soak up damage instead of relying on perfect parries.

## Crafting and Gear Progression

Gear in Reawakened is class-specific. You cannot equip heavy plate armor if you are playing a Striker or Mage. Always check the requirements on a Blueprint before spending your hard-earned materials.

1.  **Collect Recipes:** Run Gates (D-Rank or higher) to find armor and weapon recipes.
2.  **Mining:** Use a pickaxe to mine crystals found inside dungeons. These are essential for high-tier crafting.
3.  **HQ Crafting:** Visit the blacksmith at the HQ to combine your recipes and materials.
4.  **Rank Evaluation:** Once you reach the level cap for your current rank, return to the HQ to talk to the Rank Evaluator. This increases your hunter rank and allows you to take on higher-difficulty missions.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the reawakened best class for solo players?**

A: The **Assassin** is currently the best for solo play due to its high damage and ability to dodge most boss mechanics. However, the **Warrior** is a safer choice for players who struggle with the parry timing.

**Q: Can I change my class later?**

A: Yes. You can use **Class Reset** items, which are often given out via codes or available in the shop. This allows you to keep your levels while picking a new skill tree.

**Q: Why is Strength important for a Mage?**

A: In Reawakened, Strength scales the base damage of all attacks and skills. While Intelligence provides the mana needed to cast spells, Strength ensures those spells actually hit hard.

**Q: How do I get a Secret Key?**

A: Secret Keys are rare rewards from Daily Quests. You are often given a choice between +5 levels or a Secret Key. It is generally better to take the Key, as it grants access to high-tier Demon Dungeons in the late game.
