## Essential Combat Strategies

Mastering combat in Reawakened requires more than just mashing buttons. You must learn the "Animation Cancel" technique, which allows you to skip the ending frames of an attack to immediately transition into a block or a dodge. This is particularly important when facing high-level players in the Arena or tackling the endgame dungeons.

1.  **Monitor Your Stamina:** Never let your stamina bar hit zero, or you will be stunned for three seconds.
2.  **Element Matching:** Use the **reawakened roblox wiki page** to find boss weaknesses. Fire-based bosses take 50% more damage from Water-affinity skills.
3.  **Perfect Blocking:** Timing your block exactly as an attack hits will negate all damage and reflect a portion back at the attacker.
