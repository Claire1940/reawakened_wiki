## Getting Started: The Path of the Hunter

When you first step into the world of Reawakened, the game greets you with an introductory sequence that tests your reflexes. Failing these initial timed events can lead to an immediate kick from the session, so focus is required from the very first second. ### The Daily Quest System
True to the source material, the "System" imposes a daily training regime that players must complete to avoid severe penalties. Accessing this through the 'T' key, you must perform a set of physical tasks:

*   **100 Squats:** Requires rhythmic timing.
*   **100 Push-ups:** Completed through three distinct timed events.
*   **100 Sit-ups:** Standard repetition training.
*   **400 Seconds of Running:** A test of endurance across the map.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore your daily quests. Failing to complete these tasks within the allotted time will result in being teleported to the **Punishment Zone**, a dangerous area where survival is the only objective.

## Equipment: Armor and Weapons

Your gear is divided into ranks that mirror your Hunter rank. As you clear higher-level gates, you will find blueprints and materials needed to craft superior equipment. The **reawakened trello** lists specific armor sets that provide set bonuses for certain classes.

### Armor Tiers and Ranks
*   **E-Rank:** Basic clothing for new hunters.
*   **D-Rank:** Aspiring Armor (Warrior focus).
*   **C-Rank:** Arcanist Regalia (Mage focus).
*   **B-Rank:** Gilded Vestmen (Healer focus).
*   **A-Rank:** Valiant Steel Armor (High-tier Warrior gear).
*   **S-Rank:** The Dancer Armor (Elite Warrior gear with high agility).

### Weaponry
Weapons are not just stat sticks; they provide unique skill sets. For example, the **Dancing Blade** is a highly sought-after sword that allows for fluid, rapid-fire strikes. Other weapons like daggers enable the "Poison Stab" skill, which applies a damage-over-time effect to enemies.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the "Craft NPC" in the main hub after a dungeon run. Bosses drop blueprints that are required to upgrade your weapon tier from A to S.

## Dungeon Gates and World Events

The core gameplay loop involves clearing Gates. These are portals that connect the human world to dangerous dimensions. According to the **reawakened trello**, gates vary in size and difficulty, but a larger gate does not always guarantee a harder challenge—it's the rank that matters.

1.  **Locate a Gate:** Gates appear randomly in the world with a rank (E through S).
2.  **Enter the Dungeon:** You have a limited time (often around 4-5 minutes) to clear the dungeon before a "Dungeon Break" occurs.
3.  **Defeat the Boss:** Bosses have unique mechanics, such as lightning strikes or grab attacks that cannot be parried.
4.  **Claim Loot:** Blueprints and rare materials are found in the loot boxes spawned after the boss falls.

Combat in these dungeons relies heavily on the parry mechanic. Successfully parrying a boss's attack can open a window for a critical counter-attack, which is often the only way to deal significant damage to high-health S-Rank enemies.
