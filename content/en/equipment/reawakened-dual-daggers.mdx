## Ability Breakdown and Skill Rotation

The true power of the **reawakened dual daggers** lies in their active abilities. Each skill serves a specific purpose, from escaping tight corners to initiating a burst of damage on a single target.

### [G] - Windy Afterimage
This is your primary utility and mobility tool. Upon activation, the user transforms into a white mist, significantly increasing their walkspeed. During this transformation, the user releases four columns of wind.
*   **Damage:** 6 True Damage per column.
*   **Effect:** Ragdolls enemies, providing a window for follow-up attacks.
*   **Strategic Use:** Use this to reposition behind a boss or to escape a crowd of non-boss enemies when your health is low.

### [T] - Spinning Blitz
This is your "bread and butter" damage dealer. The user leaps into the air and spins the daggers in a whirlwind of steel.
*   **Initial Damage:** 25 base damage.
*   **Follow-up:** A forward-facing shockwave dealing 7.2 Piercing damage.
*   **Status Effect:** Applies **Bleed Stacks**.
*   **Strategic Use:** This move is excellent for clearing waves of enemies that have been grouped together by your teammates.

### [R] - Dagger Throw
For enemies that are too dangerous to approach or those trying to flee, the Dagger Throw provides a reliable ranged option.
*   **Mechanic:** Throws both daggers simultaneously.
*   **Damage:** 11 Piercing damage per dagger hit.
*   **Crowd Control:** Causes ragdoll and applies bleed stacks.

## Combat Mechanics: Bleed Stacks and Piercing

One of the most misunderstood aspects of the **reawakened dual daggers** is the bleed mechanic. Similar to high-tier builds in games like *Elden Ring*, bleed in Wave Defense Gaem functions as a percentage-based damage over time (DoT) effect.

When you land hits with *Spinning Blitz* or *Dagger Throw*, you build up a hidden "Bleed Meter" on the enemy. Once this meter reaches 100%, the enemy takes a massive chunk of damage regardless of their current health pool. This makes the daggers particularly effective against "bullet sponge" bosses.

## Advanced Strategies for Dual Wielding

To truly master the **reawakened dual daggers**, you must understand the rhythm of dual-wielding. Unlike heavy weapons like the Greatsword or the Bone Greatsword, daggers rely on "death by a thousand cuts."

1.  **The Hit-and-Run:** Use *Windy Afterimage* to enter the fray, ragdolling the front line. Immediately follow up with *Spinning Blitz* to apply bleed, then use your increased walkspeed to retreat before the enemies recover.
2.  **Ranged Harassment:** If a boss like the Reaper is performing a massive area-of-effect (AoE) attack, stay at the edge of the arena and spam *Dagger Throw*. This keeps the bleed meter from decaying without putting you in harm's way.
3.  **Synergy with Passives:** Look for passives that increase "Damage over Time" or "Attack Speed." Since the daggers hit multiple times during the *Spinning Blitz* animation, every point of flat damage added to your stats is multiplied significantly.

## FAQ

**Q: Are the reawakened dual daggers better than the Dual Linked Swords?**

**Q: Can I use the reawakened dual daggers in PvP?**

A: Absolutely. The ragdoll effect from *Windy Afterimage* and the ranged pressure of *Dagger Throw* make it very difficult for opponents to close the gap or escape once they have been hit by a bleed-stacking combo.
