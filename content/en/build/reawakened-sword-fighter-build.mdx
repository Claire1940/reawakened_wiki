## Choosing the Best Class for Sword Combat

While Reawakened offers six distinct classes, not every class is suited for a dedicated sword fighter. To maximize your efficiency with a blade, you must choose a class that complements physical weapon scaling and close-quarters combat.

The **Warrior** class is the gold standard for this build. It provides a balanced distribution of health, attack, and speed, making it the most versatile choice for various combat scenarios. However, if you prefer a high-risk, high-reward playstyle, the **Assassin** class offers incredible speed and combo potential, albeit at the cost of durability.

| Class | Combat Style | Primary Benefit | Skill Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warrior** | All-Rounder | Balanced health and attack | High versatility in PvE and PvP |
| **Assassin** | Speed/Burst | Extreme mobility | Long, high-damage blade combos |
| **Tank** | Frontline | Massive durability | High posture and defense |
| **Striker** | Combo-Heavy | Rapid strikes | Linking attacks for burst damage |

> **💡 Tip:** Beginners should start with the Warrior class to learn enemy patterns before transitioning into the more fragile Assassin or Striker classes for advanced gameplay.

## Optimal Stat Distribution for Sword Fighters

In Reawakened, every level grants you a stat point that can be invested into Agility, Strength, Vitality, or Intelligence. For a **reawakened sword fighter build**, your primary focus should be a split between Strength and Agility, with just enough Vitality to survive boss mechanics.

The game features a unique "Perk" system where reaching 100 points in a specific stat unlocks a game-changing ability. For sword users, reaching these milestones is non-negotiable.

### Core Stats for Sword Fighters

*   **Strength:** Increases your raw damage and, more importantly, your posture damage. At 100 Strength, you unlock a perk that significantly boosts your ability to guard-break enemies.
*   **Agility:** Enhances your movement and attack speed. Reaching 100 Agility unlocks the **Teleport** ability, which is vital for closing the gap or escaping red gate bosses.
*   **Vitality:** Increases your maximum HP. While not a primary damage stat, neglecting Vitality will lead to being one-shot in S-rank dungeons.

| Stat Milestone | Perk Unlocked | Impact on Sword Build |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **100 Strength** | Posture Mastery | Breaks enemy guards 25% faster |
| **100 Agility** | Teleport | Instant repositioning during combos |
| **100 Vitality** | Resilient Frame | Increases damage reduction against physical hits |

## The Path System: Choosing Your Focus

At the start of your journey, you must choose one of three "Paths." This decision dictates the type of content you will engage with most frequently. For a **reawakened sword fighter build**, the choice depends on whether you prefer hunting players or monsters.

1.  **Hunter Path:** This is the recommended path for most sword fighters. It focuses on subjugation missions and boss battles. Since sword builds excel at single-target damage and posture breaking, hunting bosses provides the best rewards for your effort.
2.  **Assassin Path:** If you enjoy competitive play, this path allows you to take on player contracts. The high mobility of a sword fighter makes you a nightmare for Mage or Healer classes in the open world.
3.  **Chairman Path:** Focused on PvE and resource gathering. Choose this if you prefer leading rescue missions and mining rare ores to craft high-tier gear.

## Gear and Traits: Enhancing the Blade

Your equipment is the final layer of your build. Unlike some RPGs where any gear works, Reawakened features gear specifically tailored to your chosen class. As a sword fighter, you should look for armor sets that provide bonuses to physical damage and stamina regeneration.

### Traits: The Secret Power
Traits act as secondary powers that can drastically alter your combat effectiveness. One of the most sought-after traits for a physical build is the **Goliath** trait.

*   **Goliath Trait:** Provides a massive boost to physical power and survivability. It allows a sword fighter to trade hits with bosses without being staggered.
*   **Speedster Trait:** Ideal for Assassin-based sword builds, focusing on dodge windows and movement speed.

> **Warning:** Do not ignore your daily quests! The mini-games for push-ups and squats provide essential progression that supplements your gear stats.

## Progression Strategy: From E-Rank to S-Rank

To successfully pilot a **reawakened sword fighter build**, you must follow a structured progression path. The game features eight different gate ranks, and rushing into high-rank dungeons without proper preparation will result in failure.

### Leveling Milestones

1.  **Levels 1-20 (E to D Rank):** Focus entirely on the Quest Board. Complete "Investigate" and "Clear Gate" missions to build a baseline of Strength and Agility.
2.  **Levels 21-50 (C to B Rank):** Begin focusing on your 100-point stat milestones. Prioritize Strength first to ensure you can clear the bosses in secret dungeons.
3.  **Levels 51+ (A to S+ Rank):** At this stage, your build should be finalized. Focus on farming Red Gates for legendary-tier weapons and armor that complement your Warrior or Assassin skills.

| Gate Rank | Difficulty | Recommended Build Stage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E - D Rank** | Low | Basic class skills and starter gear |
| **C - B Rank** | Medium | One stat milestone (100 pts) reached |
| **A - S Rank** | High | Two stat milestones and Goliath trait |
| **Red Gates** | Extreme | Max gear, optimized traits, and full skill tree |

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best stat to max first for a reawakened sword fighter build?**

A: It is generally recommended to max **Strength** to 100 first. The posture damage bonus is essential for breaking the guards of bosses in early to mid-game gates, allowing you to deal massive damage without being interrupted.

**Q: Can I change my class if I don't like the Warrior playstyle?**

A: Yes, the game allows for class changes, though it may require specific items or currency. If you find the Warrior too slow, switching to the **Striker** or **Assassin** while keeping your sword-focused stats is a viable way to pivot your build.

**Q: Are daily quests mandatory for progression?**

A: While not strictly mandatory, they are highly recommended. The mini-games for push-ups, sit-ups, and squats provide consistent stat growth and are one of the most reliable ways to bridge the gap between ranks without grinding gates for hours.

**Q: Which path is best for solo players?**

A: The **Hunter Path** is ideal for solo players. It focuses on subjugation missions where you fight bosses or small groups of enemies, which is perfectly suited for the high single-target damage of a sword fighter.
