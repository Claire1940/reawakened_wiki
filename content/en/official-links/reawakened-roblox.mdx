## The Daily Training Grind: How to Level Fast

Progression in **reawakened roblox** mirrors the training regimen of Sung Jin-woo. Every day, you are assigned a daily quest that involves four physical activities. Completing these is the fastest way to gain early levels and unlock the "Secret Key."

*   **Push-ups:** A rhythm-based mini-game where you must press specific keys as they enter the circle.
*   **Sit-ups:** A timing challenge where you press 'A' when the line hits the green bar.
*   **Squats:** Similar to sit-ups, requiring you to press 'E' on the blue bar.
*   **Running:** Simply move around the map until the distance requirement is met.

Once you finish these, you are often given a choice between **+5 Levels** or a **Secret Key**. Experienced players recommend choosing the **Secret Key**. While levels are easy to farm through combat, Secret Keys are rare items used for late-game "Demon Dungeons" that offer massive rewards.

## Class Tier List and Skill Requirements

Your class determines your combat style. In **reawakened roblox**, you can choose your class by pressing 'M' and navigating to the skills menu. Each class has specific point requirements to unlock powerful abilities.

### Current Meta Rankings (2026)
1.  **Tank:** Currently considered the strongest early-game class. Tanks have the highest M1 (basic attack) damage in the game, allowing them to "four-tap" opponents of equal level.
2.  **Warrior:** The most balanced option. Warriors have excellent skill combos and reliable defense, making them perfect for boss fights.
3.  **Assassin:** High burst damage but very fragile. Great if you have low ping and can land parries consistently.
4.  **Mage:** Powerful late-game but very difficult to start with. Their first two abilities are weak, making the early level 1-20 grind a nightmare.
5.  **Striker/Healer:** These are currently specialized roles. Striker is highly skill-based and difficult to master, while Healer is only viable if you are playing in a dedicated group.

## Stat Milestones and Passive Effects

In **reawakened roblox**, stats do more than just increase numbers. Reaching **100 points** in a specific stat unlocks a unique passive effect that can fundamentally change how you fight.

*   **Strength (STR):** The primary damage source for *all* classes (even Mages). At 100 points, you gain **increased posture damage**, making it easier to break an enemy's guard.
*   **Agility (AGI):** Reduces skill cooldowns. At 100 points, you unlock the **Teleport** ability, providing essential mobility for dodging boss attacks.
*   **Intelligence (INT):** Increases your mana pool. At 100 points, you unlock a passive that **restores mana on every hit**, allowing for infinite skill rotations.
*   **Vitality (VIT):** Increases health. At 100 points, you gain the ability to **block-break through parries**, a devastating tool in PvP.

> **Warning:** Do not ignore Strength even if you are a Mage. Currently, the game's scaling relies heavily on Strength for base attack damage. A balanced build of Strength and your class's primary stat is usually the most effective.

## Combat Mastery: Parrying and Dungeons

The combat system is timing-based. While you can spam M1 attacks, higher-level bosses and players will easily punish you.

1.  **Blocking vs. Parrying:** Holding 'F' blocks damage but drains your posture bar. Tapping 'F' right before an attack lands performs a **Parry**, which stuns the attacker and leaves them open for a counter-attack.
2.  **Follow-up Attacks:** Pressing **Control** after an M1 combo can trigger a follow-up attack, though this is currently noted as being slightly buggy in the 2026 playtests.
3.  **Subjugation vs. Gates:** For pure leveling, stick to **Subjugation** missions. They provide the best XP per minute. **Gates** (Dungeons) should be done for crafting materials and blueprints. You cannot get high-tier gear as direct drops; you must find the blueprint in a gate and then craft it at the HQ.

If you are struggling with the difficulty, visit the [official Roblox page](https://www.roblox.com/home) to find the community Discord. Playing in a group of 3-4 people makes Dungeons significantly faster and safer, as players can cycle aggro and heal each other.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best class for a solo player in reawakened roblox?**

A: The **Tank** or **Warrior** are the best solo classes. They have high health pools and high base damage, which allows you to survive mistakes while learning boss patterns.

**Q: How do I get better gear?**

A: Gear is obtained through crafting. You must complete "Gate" missions to find blueprints and mine crystals for materials. Take these to the blacksmith NPC at the HQ to craft your weapons and armor.

**Q: Why can't I parry attacks?**

A: Parrying is server-dependent. If your ping is over 200ms, parrying becomes extremely difficult. If you have high lag, focus on a high-Vitality "Tank" build so you can survive hits you are unable to parry.

**Q: Should I choose the Secret Key or +5 Levels?**

A: Always choose the **Secret Key**. Leveling is a fast process through Subjugation quests, but Secret Keys are rare and required for the highest-tier endgame content.
